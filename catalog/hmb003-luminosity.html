HMB003 · 2016 · cd, digital

Luminosity

Rykard

Digipak CD$19.99 USD · Buy

Luminosity (HMB003, 2016) is Rykard's second full-length for Hunya Munya, arriving six years after the 2010 debut. Fourteen tracks of ambient electronica moving between glassy headphone-first compositions and the occasional sharper rhythmic turn, with Ictis nodding toward Boards of Canada and 808 State before settling back into the record's patient core.

Mastered by Justin Moreh, with cover art by Barry Wearing. Pressed as a limited digipak CD with digital.
