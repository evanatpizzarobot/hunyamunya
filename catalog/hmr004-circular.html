HMR004 · 2005 · vinyl-12, digital

Circular

Snake Sedrick & Khans & Tim Fretwell & Yvel & Tristan & Alican & Soner & Cassino & Labèn

Circular (HMR004, 2005) is a long-form progressive-house workout from Snake Sedrick and Khans. The A side opens with a 3:30 intro that bleeds into the 8:50 original, a two-part structure designed to live on a DJ's deck rather than a home stereo.

Tim Fretwell's remix (8:32) fills the B side, pulling the track into progressive-breaks territory without losing the slow-build logic of the original. Pressed as a 12" with sleeve design by Change Design & Development UK.

Four digital-only remixes followed as companion releases, extending the track across different progressive corners. Yvel & Tristan brought their signature melodic progressive territory; the Hungarian duo's earlier remix work had landed on Global Underground 028: Shanghai (Nick Warren) and in John Digweed's Kiss 100 rotation before Snake Sedrick invited them onto HMR. Alican & Soner added a "Bended Mix" leaning deeper into Turkish progressive-house phrasing; Cassino & Labèn (HMDIGITAL010, HMDIGITAL016) contributed a "Hurry Mix" with tighter kick-forward energy; a shortened DJ Edit rounded out the set for working the floor.
