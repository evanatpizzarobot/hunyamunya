HMDIGITAL015 · 2008 · digital

International Soundscapes EP

Various Artists & Onirika & En-Tropic & Serge Govor

A six-track EP compiling work from three international producers: Onirika (Roberta Galvini, Italy), En-Tropic (Igor Jadranin), and Govor (Serge Govor, Latvia). Released on Hunya Munya Digital, January 10, 2008. The public release was digital only; CDr promos were sent out to DJs, which is why Discogs lists the format as CDr.

Styles span progressive house, electro, and tech house. Each artist contributed two tracks.
