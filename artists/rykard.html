Rykard

§ Profile · HMR

UK ambient-electronic project (Richard Wearing, Lancashire) and Hunya Munya's anchor artist. Debut Arrive the Radio Beacon (HMB001, 2010); "North Cormorant Obscurity" has passed 25 million streams with support from BBC Radio 1, 3, and 6 Music, KCRW, KEXP, and CBC Radio 2.

AmbientElectronicExperimental

RYKARD, a.k.a. Rick Wearing, is a visionary producer of ambient electronica from Preston, UK, crafting lush, cinematic soundscapes that blur the line between dream and reality. His debut album, Arrive the Radio Beacon (2010), captured the imagination of listeners worldwide with its ethereal blend of atmospheric textures, deep low-end warmth, and delicate melodic fragments. The record earned critical acclaim and support from influential tastemakers, from the BBC to KCRW in Los Angeles, cementing Rykard as a fresh and distinctive voice in electronic music.

Known for his meticulous production style and immersive arrangements, Rykard draws inspiration from nature, science fiction, and the quiet poetry of everyday life, creating music that feels both intimate and otherworldly. His compositions have resonated with fans across streaming platforms, with tracks such as “North Cormorant Obscurity” becoming underground anthems, amassing millions of plays and earning a cult following over the years.

As he continues to release music and perform, Rykard remains committed to pushing the boundaries of ambient electronica, offering listeners not just songs, but entire worlds to get lost in. His work stands as a testament to the enduring power of mood, atmosphere, and sonic storytelling.
