*Ever After* (HMDIGITAL001, 2005) is the first release on Hunya Munya Digital, the label's short-lived sublabel for MP3-era single-track releases. Jairus Miller's breaks production runs 7:44, a long patient build with the kind of arrangement that rewards headphones rather than a quick club playout.

Released as a single file at MP3 320, the kind of pre-streaming digital drop that defined the sublabel's 2005 to 2008 run.
