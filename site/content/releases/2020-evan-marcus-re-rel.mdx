*Re//Rel* (HMB006, 2020) is Evan Marcus's seven-track digital album for Hunya Munya, a loose retrospective built around older material reframed alongside new pieces. The record opens with the 10:56 "Bs As Deep Mini Edit" of Aleiana's 2005 remix of *Life Goes On*, sets up a short IDM vignette (*Evac*), and then runs through a half-Lyfe edit of *All I Could See* before returning to the original *Life Goes On* in full.

The back half pulls the record toward experimental and dark-ambient territory: *31 Seconds*, the 1:36 sketch *Rocket Ship*, and the closer *Spear* (4:04). Released as a seven-file digital album at MP3 256 kbps.
