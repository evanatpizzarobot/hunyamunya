*Re//Rel* (HMB006, 2020) is Evan Marcus's seven-track digital album on Hunya Munya Records. Opens with a ten-minute Aleiana remix of *Life Goes On* (edited by Bs As Deep), then moves through short IDM vignettes, breakbeat workouts, and dark-ambient pieces before returning to the original *Life Goes On* at full length. 7×File, MP3 256 kbps.
