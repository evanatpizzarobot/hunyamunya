*Flicker* (HMR006, 2007) is Boom Jinx's contribution to the Hunya Munya vinyl series, the Norwegian producer working in the kind of progressive territory the HMR catalog was built around.

The A side carries the Original Mix; the B side flips to a Shiloh remix, keeping the 12" single format the label's vinyl line had settled into across its first half-dozen releases.
