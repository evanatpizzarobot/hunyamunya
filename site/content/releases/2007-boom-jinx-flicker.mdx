*Flicker* (HMR006, 2007) is Norwegian producer Boom Jinx's Hunya Munya 12", a patient progressive-house piece that builds in long broad strokes across the A side, engineered for the peak-time floor rather than headphone listening.

Shiloh flips the track on the B side with a cleaner, lifted melodic take. The sixth entry in the HMR vinyl run.
