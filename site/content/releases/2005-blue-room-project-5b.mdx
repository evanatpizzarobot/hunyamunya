*5B* (HMR002, 2005) is Blue Room Project's ten-minute progressive-house anthem, a slow-building piece written and produced by Jeff Belfi and Shane Ehlers. The original runs 10:49, taking its time with the kind of patient arrangement the label's early vinyl releases were built around.

The flip carries "Madoka's Metasound Mix" (9:26), which keeps the length and reshapes the energy toward breaks. Pressed as a 12" with sleeve artwork by Change Design & Development UK and photography by Jim Sanger.
