*Chasing Memories* (HMR007, 2008) is Distant Fragment's return to the Hunya Munya catalog after the 2006 digital EP, this time on 12". The A side carries the original at 8:59, a progressive-trance piece written in the long broad arcs the vinyl series had come to favor.

The B-side flip brings in Shmuel Flash and Huge-A for a 7:20 rework that leans into electro-house, trading the original's patience for a tighter attack. Pressed and distributed by Critical Rhythm Ltd. (UK), with sleeve design by Change Design & Development.
