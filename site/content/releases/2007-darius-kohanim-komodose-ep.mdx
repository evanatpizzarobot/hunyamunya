*Komodose EP* (HMDIGITAL014, 2007) is Darius Kohanim's most ambitious Hunya Munya Digital entry: a seven-track set running past an hour, moving across techno, tech-house, and ambient without committing to any one. *October* (7:34) opens at a slower pace before the body of the record settles into a run of ten-minute pieces.

Two of those pieces carry featured contributors: V sits inside *Whothe* (10:15), and Escape on *Sacrifice* (10:16). The closing trio, *One Got Away*, *Moo Got 2*, and *Glynn*, pulls the arrangement back toward the ambient edge. Released as a seven-file drop at MP3 320.
