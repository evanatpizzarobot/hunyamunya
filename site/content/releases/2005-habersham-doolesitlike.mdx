*Doolesitlike* (HMDIGITAL005, 2005) is a ten-minute Habersham and Darius Kohanim collaboration, released the same day as *Dune In Erf Minor* as a companion piece. The track moves across progressive house, breaks, and tech-house textures, cycling through moods the way long-form tracks of the era often did.

Released on the Hunya Munya Digital sublabel as a single file at MP3 320.
