Rykard's landmark 2010 debut album *Arrive the Radio Beacon*. The record that put him on the map and started a 15-year journey that has now surpassed 25 million streams and earned support from BBC Radio, KCRW Los Angeles, CBC, and tastemaker stations around the world. Featuring the underground anthem "North Cormorant Obscurity," along with fan favorites "Monolithic," "Faustian Pact," "Winnat's Pass," and the haunting "Bowland Glider Outro Mix," this album remains the definitive Rykard experience for listeners and collectors alike.

Originally pressed by Hunya Munya Records in 2010, this album has been out of print for over a decade. We recently uncovered a very small number of sealed, mint condition original copies in the label archive.

Currently unavailable while we determine the best way to release these final original pressings to the fans who have supported Rykard over the years, as there's less than a handful remaining. When these become available, purchases will be limited to one copy per customer out of respect for the fans who have followed Rykard's journey.
