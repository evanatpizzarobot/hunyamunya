*Running After Time Remix EP* (HMDIGITAL016, 2008) is the four-track companion package to Cassino and Labèn's 2006 original EP (HMDIGITAL010), with both source tracks reworked twice. *Running After Time* gets remixes from Danny Lloyd (9:58) and Darius Kohanim (7:42); *Inventiveness Vibration* gets turned over by Michael and Levan (10:11) and Nico Grubert (7:26).

The four mixes spread across progressive house, tech house, and ambient territory, each approaching the source material from a different angle. Released on Hunya Munya Digital as a four-file drop at MP3 320.
