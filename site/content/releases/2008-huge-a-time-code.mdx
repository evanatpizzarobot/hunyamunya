*Time Code* (HMR008, 2008) is Huge-A (Adir Elimelech) delivering a progressive-house original on the A side, working the long patient build the HMR catalog had taken as a through-line.

The B side carries two Nosmo and Kris B remixes, each credited in the other's name order (a "Nosmo vs. Kris B Mix" and a "Kris B vs. Nosmo Mix"), the kind of inside joke the duo often stamped on their back-to-back reworks. Pressed as a 12" in 2008 with a CDr promo single that year and a 320 kbps digital reissue in 2011. Sleeve design by Change (UK); artwork by Adir Elimelech.
