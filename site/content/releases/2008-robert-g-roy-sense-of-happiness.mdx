*Sense Of Happiness* (HMDIGITAL018, 2008) is the last release on the Hunya Munya Digital sublabel, closing a three-year run of single-track and short-EP digital drops with a final digital single. Robert G. Roy's Original (8:03) sits in electro and tech-house territory, a pulsing minimal piece paced for longer sets.

The package carries two remixes: En-Tropic's 8:13 rework holds the original's length, and Serge Govor's 5:23 mix trims the runtime for a tighter variant. Written and produced by Robert G. Roy.
