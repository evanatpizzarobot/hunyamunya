*Revitalized EP* (HMR005, 2006) is a two-track 12" from Darius Kohanim built around a single piece worked two ways. The A side is the original, "Vital Dub" (8:38), tracking between breaks and minimal techno with a stripped-back edge.

Habersham's remix (8:22) takes the B, pulling the piece into tech-house and giving it a different sense of room. Pressed as a 12" vinyl EP with sleeve design by Change Design & Development UK.
