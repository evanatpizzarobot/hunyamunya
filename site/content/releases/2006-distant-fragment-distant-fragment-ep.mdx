*Distant Fragment EP* (HMDIGITAL009, 2006) introduces Distant Fragment to the Hunya Munya Digital roster with a two-track progressive-house release. *Kinky Serenade* (7:13) opens with a tighter arrangement; *Spell* (11:01) stretches past eleven minutes, working in the long-form tradition the sublabel had already staked out.
