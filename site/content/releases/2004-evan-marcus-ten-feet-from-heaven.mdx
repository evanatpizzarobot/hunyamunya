*Ten Feet From Heaven* (HMR003, 2004) is Evan Marcus's debut as a recording artist on his own label. The A-side original works a patient progressive-house groove, the kind of stretched-out arrangement built for the DJs who kept the genre alive in the years on either side of 2004.

Aleiana's "017 Breaks Mix" occupies the B side, leaning harder into progressive-trance territory and pulling the track into a broken-beat frame. Pressed as a 12" single.
