*Twilight* (HMR001, 2004) opened the Hunya Munya vinyl catalog and set the template the label would follow for the rest of the decade: an A-side original paired with a deeper remix on the flip. D Bajema's progressive-house take anchors the A side, patient and linear, its arrangement building in the long broad strokes the era favored.

On the B, Noel W. Sanger's "Peace On Earth Mix" pulls the track toward tribal breaks, loosening the pulse and stretching the arrangement. Pressed as a 12" in the US.
