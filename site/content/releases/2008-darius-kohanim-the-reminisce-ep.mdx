*The Reminisce EP* (HMDIGITAL017, 2008) is the largest release on the Hunya Munya Digital sublabel: an eight-track Darius Kohanim set running more than an hour, moving through breaks, downtempo, tech-house, and ambient without ever settling into one.

Two tracks get reworked inside the same package. DT's "Dub" of *Gas Sucks* (10:41) sits across from the 9:18 Original later in the tracklist, and Evan Marcus closes the EP with his "Evan Michael Mix" of *No Brainer* (4:48), a tighter echo of the version earlier on the record. Released as an eight-file drop at MP3 320.
