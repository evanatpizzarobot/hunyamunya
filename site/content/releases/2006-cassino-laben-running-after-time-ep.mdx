*Running After Time EP* (HMDIGITAL010, 2006) is a two-track progressive and tech-house release from the Italian duo Cassino and Labèn. *Inventiveness Vibration* (9:53) and the title track *Running After Time* (9:54) are near mirror lengths, each working a long-form arc familiar to the sublabel's 2006 output.

A companion remix EP followed two years later as HMDIGITAL016. Released as a two-file drop at MP3 320.
