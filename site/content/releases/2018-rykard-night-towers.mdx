*Night Towers* (HMB004, 2018) is Rykard's third full-length for Hunya Munya, an eleven-track record that moves further into cinematic, patient ambient-electronic territory while keeping the melodic instincts the project had carried since *Arrive the Radio Beacon*.

City imagery sits across the tracklist (*Capital*, *FDR Cruise*, *Nawlins*, *Mole People*, *City Star*, the title track), the record's internal geography pulling toward late-night urban ambient. Peaked at #42 on the iTunes Electronic chart in its first week of release. Mastered by Justin Moreh, with cover art by Paul Harris. Pressed as a digipak CD with digital.
