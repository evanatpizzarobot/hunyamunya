*Burton* (HMDIGITAL003, 2005) is a 6:39 downtempo piece from Darius Kohanim, his second solo entry on the Hunya Munya Digital sublabel and a quick follow-up to *Inn* three days earlier.

Where *Inn* settled into ambient stillness, *Burton* leans into a slower pulse and more rhythmic scaffolding. Released as a single file at MP3 320.
