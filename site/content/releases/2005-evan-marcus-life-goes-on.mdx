*Life Goes On* (HMDIGITAL006, 2005) is Evan Marcus's two-track progressive-house single on the Hunya Munya Digital sublabel. The 9:35 Original sets a patient, melodic pace with ambient edges; Aleiana's remix stretches the piece a bit further, landing at 9:55.

The track would later reappear in a reworked form on the *Re//Rel* retrospective (HMB006, 2020). Released as a two-file drop at MP3 320.
