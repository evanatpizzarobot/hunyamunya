*The Orange Album* (HMB002b, 2013) is Evan Marcus's fourteen-track full-length for Hunya Munya, a 44-minute record moving across ambient, breakbeat, downtempo, dark-ambient, and experimental territory without committing to any one register. Short interstitial pieces (*Bumble Bee*, *So Long My Friend*, *Sun Rays On Planet Zed*, *Do Not Listen*) sit between the longer tracks, giving the album the shape of a loose suite rather than a straight sequence.

Featured contributors appear mid-record: Kytami on *Bay Of Toven 2* (7:32) and Derick Standard on *Dark Cloud* (4:50). A hidden secret track sits between tracks 13 and 14. Released as a CD and digital album. Written and produced by Evan Marcus.
