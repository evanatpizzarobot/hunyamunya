*The Music Speaks* (HMDIGITAL012, 2007) is a three-track single from Thee-O working in minimal and tech-house territory. The Original Mix runs 7:46, joined on the release by two remixes from Bastards Of Funk and Sonic Union: the "Massive Remix" (6:06) and the "Cut It Remix" (5:46).

The two reworks trade length for attack, landing closer to peak-time floor material. Released on Hunya Munya Digital as a three-file drop at MP3 320.
