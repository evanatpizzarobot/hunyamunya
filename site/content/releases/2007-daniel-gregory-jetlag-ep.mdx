*Jetlag EP* (HMDIGITAL011, 2007) is a two-track progressive and trance release from Daniel Gregory, one of the first digital sublabel drops of the year. *Travel* (8:14) and *The Secret* (8:46) each run past the eight-minute mark, favoring a slow-build arrangement over quick hooks.

Released on Hunya Munya Digital as a two-file drop at MP3 320.
