Curtis & Dakota is a Buenos Aires house and tech-house project led by Ariel Curtis, part of the same Argentine electronic scene that produced their HMR remixer Hernán Paredes. Their HMR debut "Long Night" (HMDIGITAL013, 2007) paired the original with Paredes's eight-minute Incredible Mix. Curtis released parallel records on Bellarine Recordings the same year, including "West Coast" and "Dancing In The Darkness."
