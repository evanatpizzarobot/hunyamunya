Argentine artist working in the electronic scene. Hernán's beginnings in 1993 drew him toward electronic music; the house and techno styles of that era shaped his musical tendencies.

On Hunya Munya Digital he delivered the *Incredible Mix* of Curtis & Dakota's *Long Night* (HMDIGITAL013, 2007), an eight-minute tech-house rework paired with the original on the two-track single.
