<p><strong>Blue Room Project</strong> is the duo of <strong>Jeff Belfi and Shane Ehlers</strong>, two New York-based producers and DJs whose sound effortlessly crosses boundaries, blending progressive, tech house, breaks, and everything in between. Their music is defined by warm, organic textures and spacious, atmospheric grooves that invite listeners into a journey as much as a dancefloor experience.</p>

<p>Jeff and Shane’s partnership runs deep, friends since the 4th grade, they have shared a musical bond for most of their lives. In 1994, a simple pair of turntables sparked a fascination that would never fade. What began as crate-digging and bedroom mixing soon evolved into a full-blown passion for DJing. Their unique chemistry behind the decks, feeding off each other’s selections and seamlessly weaving vinyl into smooth yet powerful sets, quickly set them apart. They went on to mesmerize clubs in Detroit, New York, Canada, and even Tel Aviv, building an international reputation for sets that were as uplifting as they were immersive.</p>

<p>In early 2002, the duo relocated from Long Island to Manhattan, shifting their focus toward production. They cut their teeth on remixes of Everything But The Girl’s <em>Temperamental</em> and Sasha’s <em>Baja</em>, experimenting with new sounds and refining their craft. This move solidified their identity, not only elevating their DJ sets with original material but also laying the foundation for their own forward-thinking productions. With this evolution, <strong>Blue Room Project</strong> was truly born, establishing its place as a name synonymous with innovation, deep musicality, and timeless electronic sound.</p>
