<div>Christian Leevey is one of the veterans of Berlin's Techno scene.In the past time he was among the</div>
<div>
<div id="_mcePaste">so called "new faces" from the capital. Here he worked in about each and every club and</div>
<div id="_mcePaste">accomplished his resident?activity in two well?known Techno?clubs of Berlin.</div>
<div id="_mcePaste">After he had gone ahead, Leevey energetically continued going his own way. In the year of 2000, he</div>
<div id="_mcePaste">was drawn towards Hamburg where he fortified his prestige as a DJ. When he came back to Berlin</div>
<div id="_mcePaste">some years later, he vigorously strived for his task as a producer. With the wind of Northern</div>
<div id="_mcePaste">Germany in his back and a whole lot of experiences as a DJ up his sleeve, he developed his talent</div>
<div id="_mcePaste">further and this way he got down to the first releases in the year of 2007.</div>
<div>The great diversity of his musical spectrum has served as a mirror of his own development for many</div>
<div id="_mcePaste">years now. Just as he has done as a DJ, Leevey wants to make history as a producer in a similar way</div>
<div id="_mcePaste">and has already increasingly succeeded with the past releases, so that more than 20 releases can</div>
<div>be found in his discography up to this day. His focus is clearly set on the dancefloor and his skills</div>
<div>will keep on holding the listeners under his spell in the future, no matter if as a DJ or as a producer.</div>
