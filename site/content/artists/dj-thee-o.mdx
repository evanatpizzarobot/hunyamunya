DJ Thee-O is a Los Angeles producer and DJ active in the Southern California underground rave scene since 1992, founder of Biohazard Productions and Studios in 1993 and the Biohazard Digital label in 2005. Often called the Godfather of the LA rave scene, he threw his first event "Tomorrowland" in 1995 and released his first ambient record as Reef Project in 2001. His HMR contribution "The Music Speaks" (HMDIGITAL012, 2007) appeared with remixes by Bastards Of Funk and Sonic Union.
