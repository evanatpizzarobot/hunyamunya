Italian DJ and producer. She is one of the founders of LDU Records. Currently she is A&amp;R of Rax Musik.

Onirika is the alias of Italian producer Roberta Galvini. She contributed two tracks,
*Running* and *Zufriedene*, to the HMDIGITAL015 *International Soundscapes EP* on Hunya Munya
Digital in 2008.
