<p>Born in September 1985, <strong>Nick Tsitlakidis</strong> discovered electronic music at an early age when he first heard The Prodigy — a spark that ignited a lifelong passion for sound. By the age of 15 he was experimenting with loops and early productions, exploring a range of styles as he searched for his own voice.</p>

<p>His sound began to crystallize under the influence of Sasha’s <em>Airdrawndagger</em> and the <em>Global Underground</em> compilation series, which inspired him both as a DJ and as a producer. In early 2005, Nick sought professional feedback on his work, sending tracks to Andrew K and Kosmas Epsilon, both of whom offered high praise and encouragement. This gave Nick the confidence to pursue labels for release.</p>

<p>After early digital releases on <strong>Hunya Munya Records</strong> and <strong>Fiberline Audio</strong>, Nick achieved a breakthrough with <em>Utopia</em> — a vinyl collaboration with Steve Mill released on <strong>T.U.S.O.M (The Underground Sound of Madrid)</strong>. The track earned strong support from international names such as Sultan and was later featured on the <strong>Beat Freak Annual Collection</strong>, highlighting the year’s most successful releases from the Beat Freak Label Group.</p>

<p>With more original productions and remixes on the way for labels including <strong>Hunya Munya</strong>, <strong>Underground Lessons</strong>, <strong>Fiberline Audio</strong>, <strong>Segment</strong>, and others, Nick Tsitlakidis continues to evolve as both a DJ and producer — crafting forward-thinking electronic music that bridges underground depth with global appeal.</p>
