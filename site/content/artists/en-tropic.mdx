En-Tropic was an early alias of Belgrade-born producer Igor Jadranin, who began his career in the early 2000s as a DJ before turning to production and sound design. His first record landed on the New York label HMSP Music in 2006, and in 2008 he founded the independent label Rezonant.

For Hunya Munya Digital he contributed *Electric Dancing* and *When Summer Comes* to the HMDIGITAL015 *International Soundscapes EP* (2008) and remixed Robert G. Roy's *Sense Of Happiness* (HMDIGITAL018, 2008). In 2011 he stepped out from behind his pseudonyms and launched Bright Spark Records under his own name, with later releases on Bokhari, Star Creature, and BBE Music drawing support from Gilles Peterson, Kerri Chandler, Richie Hawtin, and Laurent Garnier.
