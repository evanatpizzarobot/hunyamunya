Istanbul duo Alican Yuksel and Soner Ince started producing drum and bass and breaks out of their basement in 2000, shaped by The Prodigy, Sasha, Nick Warren, and Dave Seaman. A 2003 introduction to Murat Uncuoglu, Turkey's most influential progressive DJ and producer, turned into a mentorship and studio collaboration that accelerated their sound.

First signings came in 2004 to Dub Coast, Blue-Room Records, and Toes In The Sand. Two remixes they cut for Murat Uncuoglu on Blue-Room drew support from John Digweed on Kiss FM, Hernan Cattaneo at Creamfields, Sasha, and Nick Warren. Collaborative work on *Beyond Dreams* landed on Tao Recordings and the *Back 2 Back Sessions* compilation for Swift. A remix of Jay West's *The Way You Walk* on Spleen Records picked up Cattaneo, Warren, and Anthony Pappa. Nick Warren then signed their original *Dualities* to his Hope Recordings imprint for a 2006 release.

Co-founders of the Teknikal Rotation label with Murat Uncuoglu. Hosted a weekly two-hour slot on Turkey's FG 93.7 that pulled guest mixes from Dave Seaman, James Zabiela, Steve Porter, Kasey Taylor, Max Graham, Quivver, MV, Glenn Morrison, Shiloh, and more; a companion *Teknikal Rotation* show ran monthly on Frisky Radio.

Their "Bended Mix" of Snake Sedrick & Khans's *Circular* for Hunya Munya Records sat inside this run of output, arriving on the digital companion set that accompanied the 2005 HMR004 vinyl.
