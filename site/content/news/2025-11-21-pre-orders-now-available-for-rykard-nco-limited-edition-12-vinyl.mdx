<p>Celebrating 15 years of Rykard’s landmark track <em>“North Cormorant Obscurity”</em> from the 2010 album <em>Arrive the Radio Beacon</em>. This special anniversary edition brings the fan-favorite ambient classic to vinyl for the first time. Also featuring an unreleased companion track, “Troup Head,” on Side B.</p>

<p><strong>Tracklist &amp; Runtimes:</strong></p>

<p><strong>Side A:</strong> <em>North Cormorant Obscurity</em>, 3:20</p>

<p><strong>Side B:</strong> <em>Troup Head</em>, 5:33</p>

<p>Pressed in a <strong>limited run of 500 copies</strong>, including <strong>75 white-label promos</strong> and <strong>two</strong>&nbsp;<strong>collectible sticker designs</strong>, each vinyl includes one of the two stickers inside the sleeve.</p>
