Rykards debut album "Arrive the Radio Beacon" was just accepted into Pandora Radio! The final process takes 1-2 months and then Rykard will have his own station and be in rotation on Pandora.  Thanks for all the support, from KCRW, Anne Litt, Jason Bentley, Morning Becomes Eclectic, KALX, KEXP, BBC Radio 1, BBC Radio 3, BBC Radio Bristol, CBC Radio 2 and many more!
